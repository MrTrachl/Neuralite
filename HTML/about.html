About Neuralite

Our Vision

Neuralite is a AI centered software application designed to produce study content and exams to further everyone's education and increase their knowledge, whilst also rewarding users through a extremely unique system by monetizing users who engage and excel in their pursuit for more and more knowledge.

Monetization

Neuralite pays its users to become smarter.

Seriously.

Neuralite has embraced rewarding users with convertable XP to actual cash. We utilize crypto currencies on the block chain as a reward for users based on the level of engagement users have. In other words, if you master concepts and test out a lot, you will make a lot too.

The harder the exam, the bigger the cashback.
The longer you hold XP, the bigger the cashback multiplier.
It's that simple.

XP can be converted at any time. Upon time of conversion you must hold the conversion for 3 days for clearance and then its withdrawable to any account!

Cheating

Since Neuralite is monetized, cheating (eww) was definitely a concern. Which is why Neuralite requires access to two things:

1. Browser control
2. Your local system camera & microphone

Don't worry though! We relinquish control automatically after every exam is completed. The browser control is maintained so that if you leave the page the exam will automatically be disqualified from recieving payment, but is still completable! The camera control, likewise, is maintained for our ai algorithm safety check to ensure that you aren't glancing from the screen super often, nor are you recieving audible help!

Learning

To make learning, at a low, medium, or high level, enjoyable, attainable, and rewarding. Whether used for school, work, college, or to just learn something new, we strive to make the experience as engaging and friendly as possible for you, the user and learner.
